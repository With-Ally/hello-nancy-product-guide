=== Hello Nancy Brand Guidelines ===

TONE:
- Friendly, warm, approachable
- Beginner-friendly: no jargon, no intimidation
- Non-shaming: normalize self-exploration
- Playful: lighthearted, fun, never serious or clinical

VISUALS:
- Soft pinks, creams, pastels
- Minimal and clean design
- Discreet packaging and branding
- Fruit-inspired playful aesthetics
- Vegetable-inspired playful aesthetics
- Cute and stationary

PRODUCT FOCUS:
- Self-love and personal exploration
- Education and confidence-building
- Simple to use, no learning curve
- Beginner-friendly features and power levels

PRODUCTS TO AVOID:
- Anything too explicit or aggressive in naming or design
- Clinical or medical-feeling products
- Overly advanced or intimidating toys
- Products that require prior experience
- Anything that contradicts discreet, shame-free values
